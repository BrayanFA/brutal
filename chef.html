Cooking Recipe

Steps to do Pasta with bacon

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 2 red peppers
  • 120 g bacon
  • 1 can(450 g) tomatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • oregano
  • 50 g pasta per person

Method

  1. Cut the onion,red peppers and bacon into small pieces
  2. Heat some olive oil in a pen and fry the onion,red peppers and bacon
  3. Add oregano, garlic, tomatoes and water and cook for 20 minutes
  4. Cook the paste in a big pot of boiling water
  5. Serve the paste with the sauce, and enjoy!
